# 🌌 Echoes of the Unseen

## What Humanity Forgets, We Remember.

Echoes of the Unseen is an AI-powered heritage intelligence platform that discovers, predicts, and preserves endangered cultures, traditions, languages, oral histories, and digital artifacts before they disappear forever.

Rather than simply archiving history, Echoes actively identifies what humanity is at risk of losing and helps preserve it for future generations.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌍 Vanishing Earth

Interactive 3D globe visualizing endangered traditions, languages, communities, and cultural practices.

### 🔮 Future Historian

Upload an artifact and receive an analysis from the perspective of a historian in the year 2126.

### 📉 Heritage Risk Engine

Generates:

* Extinction Risk
* Cultural Value
* Preservation Score
* Future Relevance
* Community Importance

### 🎙️ Last Voices

Extracts stories, wisdom, traditions, and knowledge from elder recordings.

### 🌌 Echo Oracle

Answers:

"What is humanity forgetting today?"

and predicts emerging cultural losses.

### 🌠 Memory Constellation

Visual graph connecting stories, traditions, people, communities, and artifacts.
